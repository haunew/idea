## 🔬 Five-Stage Technical Details

### Stage 1: Room Wireless (2025-2030)

**Technical Principle**: Magnetic Resonant Coupling
- Frequency: 6.78 MHz (ISM band)
- Power: 10-100W
- Distance: 5-10 meters
- Efficiency: 70-85%

**System Components**:
```
Power Router (wall-plug)
    ↓ Magnetic resonance
Phone/Laptop/Headphones (receiver coil)
```

**Commercialization**: $99/device, similar to WiFi router

**Key Breakthroughs**:
- Multi-device simultaneous charging
- No precise alignment needed
- Penetrates non-metal obstacles

---

### Stage 2: Building Wireless (2030-2035)

**Technical Principle**: RF + Infrared Hybrid
- RF: 2.4/5.8GHz, wide coverage, low power
- IR: 800-1000nm, directional, high power
- Magnetic resonance: near-field, high efficiency

**System Components**:
```
Building Central Energy System
    ├─ RF broadcast: whole-building background power
    ├─ IR array: directional active charging
    └─ Smart management: AI power scheduling
```

**Building Integration**:
- New construction: embedded antennas (walls, ceilings)
- Retrofit: surface-mounted emitter panels

**Commercialization**: $5,000/home, building standard

---

### Stage 3: Region Wireless (2035-2045)

**Technical Principle**: Microwave Power Transfer (MPT) + Millimeter Wave
- MPT: 2.45/5.8GHz, kilometer range, high power
- mmWave: 28/60GHz, precise, high bandwidth
- Phased array: beamforming, dynamic tracking

**System Components**:
```
Regional Energy Center (MW scale)
    ├─ MPT base stations: wide coverage
    ├─ mmWave base stations: precise coverage
    ├─ Relay network: bypass obstacles
    └─ Smart scheduling: multi-user coordination
```

**Coverage Capability**:
- Radius: 1-10km
- Power: kW-MW level
- Support: mobile devices, electric vehicles

**Commercialization**: $9M/base station, similar to 5G network

---

### Stage 4: Global Wireless (2045-2060)

**Technical Principle**: Space-Based Solar Power (SBSP)
- Collection: GEO orbit solar array (km² scale)
- Conversion: DC → Microwave
- Transmission: Microwave beam → Ground
- Reception: Rectenna (Rectifying Antenna)

**System Components**:
```
Space Segment: GEO satellite (5-10km² solar array)
    ↓ Microwave (2.45GHz)
Transmission: Through atmosphere (10-20% loss)
    ↓
Ground Segment: Rectenna array (km² scale)
    ↓
Grid/Direct supply
```

**Key Metrics**:
- Single satellite power: 1-5GW
- Coverage: Global
- Availability: 99% (24 hours)
- LCOE: $0.024/kWh (lowest)

**Commercialization**: $25B/satellite, Energy-as-a-Service

---

### Stage 5: New Energy Era (2060+)

**Research Directions**:

| Direction | Theoretical Basis | Current Status | Potential |
|-----------|------------------|----------------|-----------|
| **Zero-Point Energy** | Quantum vacuum fluctuations | Casimir effect verified | Theoretically infinite |
| **Dark Energy** | Cosmic acceleration | Nature unknown | 68% of universe |
| **Gravitational Waves** | General relativity | Detected, hard to use | Enormous energy |

**Technical Challenges**:
- Extremely low efficiency (breakthrough needed)
- Incomplete theory
- Difficult experimental verification

**Civilization Significance**:
- If achieved: Type II or even Type III civilization
- Completely free energy
- Interstellar travel becomes possible
